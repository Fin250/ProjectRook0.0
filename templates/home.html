Welcome to Luna Moth

A warm welcome from Lisa at Luna Moth, your community face to face space for transformation and healing. As a trained menstrual cycle awareness mentor and facilitator with Red School, I’m offering a monthly 7 session sequential course starting 2nd May 2025, with a taster on 4th April 2025.

I’m a trained Red School menstruality mentor, having completed the Menstruality Leadership programme in 2022 and Menopause the Great Awakener trainings in 2023 and 2024. I am an active voluntary contributor to their graduate online community. I’ve organised an in person regional meet up for graduates and participate in regular trainings and facilitation CPD.

I specialise in creating a serene environment where you can focus on growth and healing. Let’s work together to create positive change in your life.

My Services

Menopause Support

Offering personalized guidance and support to help navigate the physical and emotional challenges of menopause. Whether through one-on-one consultations or group workshops, I provide tools and techniques to empower you through this transition.

Menstrual Cycle Awareness

A face-to-face, intimate circle for women to deepen into menstruality and conscious menopause transition. Each session includes guided meditation and embodied reflection, supporting transformation, healing, and awareness.

Menoyoga™

Yoga as a method to manage menopause: Following a 30-hour Menoyoga™ training, as an add-on to my existing long-term yoga practice, I can offer solar and lunar sequences specifically designed to balance the nervous system and develop strength.

Tarot Reading

Private face to face and online intuitive readings of up to 1 hour £30. If you refer a friend then any future readings for you are £25 by way of thank you.
